# Marketing-Campaign_Analytics-Excel
Marketing campaign performance analysis using Excel dashboards and KPIs

# Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze marketing campaign performance across multiple channels, companies, and customer segments to identify high-performing strategies. The analysis focuses on understanding conversion behavior, ROI, engagement levels, seasonal trends, and profitability to support data-driven marketing decisions and campaign optimization.

Data cleaning and preprocessing
Calculated columns (ROI, conversion rate, profit, engagement level)

# Key Insights

1. Email marketing emerged as the most effective channel with the highest average conversion rate, closely followed by website campaigns, indicating strong user intent in direct communication channels.

2. Influencer and display campaigns delivered the highest average ROI, showing that brand visibility and influencer trust play a major role in driving profitable outcomes.

3. Tech Corporations and Alpha Innovations contributed the highest share of total profit, suggesting consistent performance and effective campaign execution by these companies.

4. The majority of campaigns fall under medium to high engagement levels, indicating that most marketing efforts are resonating well with the target audience.

5. Revenue to profit flow analysis shows minimal leakage, highlighting efficient cost management across campaigns.

6. Campaign performance peaked around September, suggesting seasonal factors significantly influence customer engagement and conversion behavior.

7. Search and influencer campaigns achieved strong impressions and clicks simultaneously, reflecting effective targeting and high audience relevance.

8. Customer segments such as Tech Enthusiasts and Foodies generated higher revenue and ROI, making them key segments for future targeted marketing strategies.

# Conclusion:

The marketing campaign analysis highlights strong overall performance with efficient budget utilization across channels. Email and Website campaigns deliver the highest conversion rates, while Influencer and Display campaigns generate the strongest ROI. Key companies such as Tech Corporations and Alpha Innovations contribute the majority of total profit, indicating consistent campaign execution. Customer engagement levels remain predominantly medium to high, reflecting effective targeting. Seasonal trends show peak performance around September, emphasizing the importance of timing in marketing strategies. Overall, the analysis identifies clear opportunities to scale high-performing channels and customer segments for improved future outcomes.
